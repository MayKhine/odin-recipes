Lentil soup

Description

This is a healthy and warm soup. Enjoy as a starter for dinner or anytime of the day.

Ingredients

  • 200 g red lentils
  • 2 minced colves garlic
  • 2 minced tsp ginger
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 diced potato
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1l water
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • olive oil (for frying)

Steps

  1. Peel and mince garlic and ginger. Peel and finely dice onion. Peel and roughly dice potato. Add garlic, ginger, and onion together with olive oil to a pot, add turmeric, and saut√© over medium heat.
  2. Wash the red lentil and add it to the pot. Fry it for 2 mins or unitl its yellow color becomes even brighter
  3. add water and diced potatoes
  4. When the soup starts simmering, season with salt and pepper
  5. Cook until potatos are soft, then serve immediately.
