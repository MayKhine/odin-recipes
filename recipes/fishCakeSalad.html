Fish cake salad

Description

This popular Burmese salad is packed with traditional and refreshing flavours.

Ingredients

  • 1 Asian red eschalot or medium onion, thinly sliced, soaked in water for 10 minutes, drained
  • 2 tomatoes, seeded, chopped
  • 2 tsp finely crushed roasted peanuts
  • 1 tsp chickpea (besan) flour or bean powder
  • 1/2 coriander sprigs
  • 2 tsp fried Asian red eschalots
  • 2 tsp tamarind dipping sauce

    • Fish cake ingredients

  • 250 firm white fish fillet, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp finely chopped ginger
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1/4 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 Asian red eschalot or medium onion
  • vegetable oil to deep fry

Steps

  1. Place fish, ginger, garlic, turmeric and onion in a food processor and process to a smooth paste. Season with salt. Shape mixture into 2 in rounds.
  2. Fry the fish cakes in oil until crisp and golden
  3. Slice fish cake and place in a bowl. Add remaining ingredients; tomato, peanut, bean powder, coriander, fried onion. Gentaly toss the ingredients to combine before serving.
